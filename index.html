Brain Teaser Quiz

Test your logic, lateral thinking, and puzzle-solving skills. Can you outsmart these riddles?

Riddle #1

Question

I have keys but no locks. I have space but no room. You can enter, but you can’t go outside. What am I?

Answer

A Keyboard

Riddle #2

Question

I have cities, but no houses. I have mountains, but no trees. I have water, but no fish. What am I?

Answer

A Map

Riddle #3

Question

I speak without a mouth and hear without ears. I have no body, but I come alive with the wind. What am I?

Answer

An Echo

Riddle #4 (The Dark Turn)

Question

A woman shoots her husband, then holds him underwater for 5 minutes. Finally, she hangs him. Right after, they enjoy a lovely dinner together. How?

Answer

She took his picture.

(Shooting = taking photo, underwater/hanging = developing film in a darkroom)

Riddle #5

Question

I am an odd number. Take away one letter, and I become even. What number am I?

Answer

Seven

(Remove the "S" from Seven, and it becomes "Even")

Riddle #6

Question

What has hands but cannot clap?

Answer

A Clock

Riddle #7

Question

What gets wetter the more it dries?

Answer

A Towel

Riddle #8

Question

I have branches, but no fruit, trunk, or leaves. What am I?

Answer

A Bank

Riddle #9

Question

What can run but never walks, has a mouth but never talks, has a head but never weeps, has a bed but never sleeps?

Answer

A River

Riddle #10

Question

A grandfather, two fathers, and two sons went fishing. They caught three fish, one for each. How is this possible?

Answer

There were 3 people.

(A grandfather, his son, and his grandson)

Riddle #11

Question

What can travel around the world while staying in a single corner?

Answer

A Stamp

Riddle #12

Question

I add five to nine, and get two. The correct answer is not fourteen. How is this possible?

Answer

You are using a clock.

(9 o'clock + 5 hours = 2 o'clock)
